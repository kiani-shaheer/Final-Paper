A Societal Mission

Two years degree program in computer science attracts the participants who are seeking a fast track entry into the work force, planning to continue to four years degree program, or exploring different specializations in order to clarify their careers goals.
The primary objectives of two years degree program in computer science are to let the participants learn the theoretical and mathematical foundations of computing and scientific principles underlying computing and information technology.
